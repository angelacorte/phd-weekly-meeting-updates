Period abroad infos/iter

  • If I already know the period, I have to send the request form to Bartolini and then to AFORM.
  • The Marco Polo program is a separate thing — first, I need authorization from the others.
  • In Bartolini’s document, I have to summarize in about 4 lines what I will do during the period abroad, then she approves it.
  • At that point, I have to fill out the form available on the university intranet for PhD student mobility abroad and also the one that entitles me to the 50% scholarship increase; once this is authorized, everything is set.
  • When the Marco Polo call opens (usually in December), I need to write a project proposal and complete the procedure on the website to apply for the call.
  • When I come back, I have to write a final project report to attach to the Marco Polo call documents, and then fill in the return form to be sent to AFORM.
